## Overview

CourtSide tournaments can use three different formats:

- **Pool Play**: Teams play round-robin within pools
- **Bracket Play**: Single-elimination tournament
- **Hybrid**: Pool play followed by bracket elimination

This guide helps you navigate and understand each format.

## Viewing Pool Standings

### Accessing Pool Standings

1. Open a tournament
2. Select a division
3. Tap the "Pools & Brackets" tab
4. View the "Pools" section

### Understanding Pool Standings

Each pool displays:
- **Team Name**: Tap to view team details
- **W-L Record**: Wins and losses (e.g., "3-1")
- **Points For**: Total points scored by the team
- **Points Against**: Total points scored against the team
- **Point Diff**: Difference between points for and against

### How Teams Are Ranked

Teams in pools are ranked by:
1. **Wins** (most wins = higher rank)
2. **Point Differential** (if wins are tied)
3. **Head-to-head result** (if still tied)

### Pool Play Features

- **Expandable Pools**: Tap pool name to expand/collapse standings
- **Real-time Updates**: Standings update automatically as games complete
- **Advancement Indicators**: Teams advancing to brackets are highlighted
- **Game Count**: Shows how many games each team has played

### Example Pool Standings

```
Pool A Standings
1. Team Alpha      3-0  (+45)  150-105
2. Team Beta       2-1  (+12)  142-130
3. Team Gamma      1-2  (-18)  128-146
4. Team Delta      0-3  (-39)  115-154
```

## Viewing Brackets

### Accessing Brackets

1. Open a tournament
2. Select a division
3. Tap the "Pools & Brackets" tab
4. View the "Brackets" section

### Understanding Bracket Structure

Brackets show:
- **Round Names**: Finals, Semifinals, Quarterfinals, etc.
- **Matchups**: Which teams play each other
- **Scores**: Results of completed games
- **TBD Teams**: Teams not yet determined (waiting for previous games)

### Bracket Rounds

Different bracket sizes have different rounds:

**4-Team Bracket:**
- Semifinals (2 games)
- Finals (1 game)

**8-Team Bracket:**
- Quarterfinals (4 games)
- Semifinals (2 games)
- Finals (1 game)

**16-Team Bracket:**
- Round 1 (8 games)
- Quarterfinals (4 games)
- Semifinals (2 games)
- Finals (1 game)

**32-Team Bracket:**
- Round 1 (16 games)
- Round 2 (8 games)
- Quarterfinals (4 games)
- Semifinals (2 games)
- Finals (1 game)

### Reading Bracket Games

Each bracket game shows:
- **Teams**: Names of competing teams (or "TBD" if not determined)
- **Score**: Final score if game is completed
- **Status**: Scheduled, In Progress, or Completed
- **Time**: Scheduled start time
- **Court**: Location and court number

### Advancement

- Winners automatically advance to the next round
- Losers are eliminated from the bracket
- Next round games update in real-time as games complete

## Division Standings

### Accessing Division Standings

1. Open a tournament
2. Select a division
3. Tap the "Standings" tab

### What Division Standings Show

Overall standings for all teams in the division:
- **Rank**: Overall position (1 = first place)
- **Team Name**: Tap to view team details
- **Record**: Wins-Losses (e.g., "5-2")
- **Points For**: Total points scored
- **Points Against**: Total points allowed
- **Point Diff**: Net point differential

### How Division Standings Work

- Includes all games (pool, bracket, and standalone)
- Updated in real-time as games complete
- Sorted by wins, then point differential
- Shows complete tournament performance

### Example Division Standings

```
Division Standings
1. Team Alpha      5-1  (+67)  312-245
2. Team Beta       4-2  (+34)  289-255
3. Team Gamma      4-2  (+12)  276-264
4. Team Delta      3-3  (-8)   251-259
5. Team Epsilon    2-4  (-45)  234-279
6. Team Zeta       0-6  (-60)  198-258
```

## Team Details

### Accessing Team Details

Tap any team name anywhere in the app:
- Pool standings
- Bracket matchups
- Division standings
- Game cards
- Search results

### Team Detail Page Sections

#### 1. Team Header
- Team name
- Overall record (W-L)
- Follow button (tap to follow/unfollow)

#### 2. Statistics
- **Games Played**: Total completed games
- **Wins/Losses**: Win-loss record
- **Points For**: Total points scored
- **Points Against**: Total points allowed
- **Point Differential**: Net difference

#### 3. Game History
- Chronological list of all games
- Most recent games first
- Shows:
  - Opponent name
  - Final score
  - Result (W/L)
  - Date and time
  - Game label (pool/bracket context)

### Using Team Details

- **Follow Team**: Tap follow button to get notifications
- **View Game**: Tap any game to see full details
- **Share**: Share team page with others
- **Track Progress**: Monitor team's tournament journey

## Following Teams

### How to Follow a Team

1. Navigate to team detail page
2. Tap the "Follow" button
3. Button changes to "Following"

### Benefits of Following

- **Notifications**: Get alerts when team's games start or complete
- **Quick Access**: Followed teams appear in your profile
- **Score Updates**: Receive real-time score updates
- **Advancement Alerts**: Notified when team advances in bracket

### Managing Followed Teams

1. Go to your Profile
2. Tap "Manage Following"
3. View all followed teams
4. Tap "Unfollow" to stop following

### Following Multiple Teams

- Follow as many teams as you want
- Useful for tracking multiple teams in same tournament
- Great for parents, coaches, or fans

## Understanding Game Labels

### Pool Game Labels

Format: `[Pool Name] Game [Number]`

Examples:
- "Pool A Game 1"
- "Pool B Game 5"
- "Gold Pool Game 3"

### Bracket Game Labels

Format: `[Bracket Name] [Round Name] Game [Number]`

Examples:
- "Gold Bracket Finals"
- "Championship Semifinals Game 1"
- "Silver Bracket Quarterfinals Game 2"

### Why Labels Matter

- **Context**: Understand what stage of tournament
- **Importance**: Finals are more critical than early rounds
- **Following**: Easier to find specific games
- **Scheduling**: Know when to arrive for important games

### Label Colors and Badges

- **Pool Games**: Blue badge
- **Bracket Games**: Gold badge
- **Finals**: Red badge with special styling
- **Completed**: Gray badge

## Tips for Users

### Staying Updated

- **Enable Notifications**: Get alerts for followed teams
- **Refresh Regularly**: Pull down to refresh standings
- **Check Schedule**: View upcoming games in schedule tab
- **Bracket Updates**: Check bracket after each round completes

### Understanding Tournament Flow

**Pool-Only Tournament:**
1. All teams play round-robin in pools
2. Champion determined by final standings
3. No elimination

**Bracket-Only Tournament:**
1. Teams seeded into bracket
2. Single-elimination games
3. Winner advances, loser eliminated
4. Champion wins finals

**Hybrid Tournament:**
1. Pool play (day 1)
2. Top teams advance to bracket
3. Bracket play (day 2)
4. Champion wins bracket finals

### Common Questions

**Q: Why does a game show "TBD"?**
A: The teams haven't been determined yet. Previous games must complete first.

**Q: How often do standings update?**
A: Standings update automatically within seconds of a game being completed.

**Q: Can I see past tournament results?**
A: Yes, completed tournaments remain viewable with final standings and results.

**Q: What if two teams have the same record?**
A: Teams are ranked by point differential. If still tied, head-to-head result is used.

**Q: How do I know which teams advance from pools?**
A: Advancing teams are highlighted in pool standings. Check tournament rules for advancement count.

**Q: Can I watch games live?**
A: The app shows real-time score updates. Check tournament info for live streaming links if available.

## Glossary

- **Pool**: Group of teams playing round-robin
- **Bracket**: Single-elimination tournament structure
- **Seed**: Position/ranking in a bracket
- **Bye**: Automatic advancement to next round (no game)
- **TBD**: To Be Determined (team not yet known)
- **Point Differential**: Points scored minus points allowed
- **Advancement**: Moving from pools to brackets
- **Elimination**: Being knocked out of bracket
- **Round-Robin**: Format where each team plays every other team once
